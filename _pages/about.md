Summary
------
Dr. Zhuoye Zhang is currently a Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering (AAE) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Prior to this, he was respectively a Research Associate at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) and a Postdoctoral Fellow at The University of Hong Kong (HKU). He received his B.Eng. in Transportation Engineering from Tongji University under the supervision of [Prof. Wei Zhu](https://tjjt.tongji.edu.cn/info/3028/10903.htm), a M.Eng. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University under the supervision of [Prof. Linjun Lu](https://en.naoce.sjtu.edu.cn/teachers/LuLinjun.html), and a Ph.D. in Industrial Engineering from The University of Hong Kong under the supervision of [Prof. Fangni Zhang](https://www.dase.hku.hk/people/f-n-zhang). Dr. Zhang’s research covers a wide range of fundamental and emerging issues in transportation, including shared and automated transport management, multimodal transportation system modelling and optimization and network modeling and analysis. His research has been published in world-leading journals and conferences in the field, e.g., Transportation Research Part A/B/C/E, International Symposium of Transportation and Traffic Theory (ISTTT). He served as a visiting student at University College London in 2019 under the supervision of [Prof. Taku Fujiyama](https://profiles.ucl.ac.uk/9077-taku-fujiyama), and at the University of Michigan in 2024 under the supervision of [Prof. Yafeng Yin](https://cee.engin.umich.edu/people/yin-yafeng/), respectively.
